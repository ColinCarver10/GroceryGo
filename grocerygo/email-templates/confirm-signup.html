Once you've confirmed your account, here's how to get started:

1. Log in — Use the email and password you signed up with.

3. Go to "Generate Plan" — Click it in the navigation bar or "Create Your First Meal Plan" on your dashboard.

4. Select your meals — Click the slots you want (breakfast, lunch, dinner) or use shortcuts like "Weekday Lunches Only" or "Dinners Only."

5. Generate — Click "Generate Meal Plan." We'll create personalized recipes and a shopping list based on your preferences.
